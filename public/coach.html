Our Coaches

Duvvarapu SivaKumar

A distinguished member of the coaching team at Elite Cricket Academy, celebrated for his extensive international and domestic cricketing background. Born in India and making a significant mark as a right-handed bowling all-rounder, Siva Kumar's journey from playing for the Andhra cricket team to representing the United States on the international stage exemplifies his versatile talent and dedication to the sport. A pivotal figure in the India Under-19 team that clinched the 2008 ICC Under-19 Cricket World Cup, and later shining in India's domestic circuits before transitioning to the USA cricket scene, his experiences bring a rich perspective to coaching. With an ICC Level 1 coaching certification, Siva Kumar specializes in honing the technical and mental aspects of young cricketers, ensuring they are well-prepared for the complexities of competitive cricket. His approach is deeply rooted in fostering discipline, teamwork, and resilience, aiming to elevate the game of each athlete at Elite Cricket Academy.

David Walker

Enriches Elite Cricket Academy with his profound expertise as an ECC Level 2 certified coach, bringing a wealth of experience from his impactful tenure at Uxbridge Cricket Club. Announced as the Cricket Development Manager in January 2018, David's prior role as a staff player with Middlesex CCC laid the foundation for his successful coaching career, particularly highlighted by coaching the U13 team to triumph at the County Finals Day. His appointment enabled him to extend the winning strategies and coaching framework across all club levels, significantly uplifting the cricketing standards and community engagement. David's strategic vision for cricket development, coupled with his initiative to expand the coaching team, notably by including England Over 60's player Mike Stafford, showcases his dedication to nurturing young talent. At Elite Cricket Academy, David's innovative coaching approach and commitment to excellence continue to inspire and develop aspiring cricketers, making him a pivotal figure in our coaching team.

Harshad Joshi

A cornerstone of Elite Cricket Academy's coaching faculty, bringing unparalleled insights from his illustrious journey in Ranji cricket and his influential role as a BCCI selector for Saurashtra. His tenure on the domestic front as a player was marked by dedication, skill, and a profound understanding of cricket dynamics, which he seamlessly transitioned into a knack for spotting and nurturing emerging talent in his selector role. Joshi's approach to coaching is deeply influenced by his rich playing and administrative experience, emphasizing a blend of technical mastery, strategic thinking, and psychological resilience. Under his guidance, young cricketers at the academy are exposed to the rigors of professional cricket, equipped not just with skills but with the mindset to navigate the challenges of the sport. His commitment to fostering young talent and his ability to inspire and motivate make him a pivotal figure in shaping the future stars of cricket.
